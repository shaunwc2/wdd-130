Why we meet

Ever since 1995 we have been gathering for 3 days a year in July. The first reunion was held because Grandma and Grandpa were on their mission to New Zeland and the siblings wanted to make sure they would still get together. The only year that has been missed was in 2020 during the covid pandemic. Since the first reunion our numbers have grown by over 110 people. We haven't gotten sick of each other yet and are looking forward to many more years.
